OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive, dignified, and professional solutions tailored to honour your loved ones and support your family.

Tombstone Design & Manufacturing

Our unique range of affordable, high-quality tombstones come in various colors and designs, allowing you to select a unique creation that suits your needs and traditional burial customs.

  • Customized tombstones crafted from high-quality materials
  • SABS specs compliant, designed to withstand the elements
  • Personalized designs including engravings, photographs, and epitaphs
  • Various styles, finishes, and markers for gravesites

Funeral Planning & Arrangements

We assist families in planning and organizing complete funeral services. Our professional and compassionate staff will help coordinate all aspects to ensure a seamless process.

  • Full-service planning tailored to cultural and religious preferences
  • Coordination with cemeteries, crematoriums, and service providers
  • Provision of high-quality Caskets, Coffins, and Urns
  • Hearse and Van coordination
  • Embalming room equipment and professional preparation

Exhumation Services

We handle the delicate process of exhumation with compassion, respect, and expertise, whether for relocation or further examination.

  • Legal consultation and assistance obtaining necessary permits
  • Expert team utilizing proper equipment and respectful techniques
  • Guidance and support for reburial services
  • Full documentation assistance (updated death certificates)
  • Clear, sensitive communication and cost transparency

Memorial & Catering Services

We help organize memorial services and celebrations of life, personalized to reflect the interests of the deceased, providing a meaningful way for family to gather.

  • Traditional funeral services (visitation, ceremony, graveside)
  • Cremation services with personalized urn options
  • Supply of tents, tables, and chairs
  • Mobile toilets and VIP toilets
  • Professional decorations, foods, and drinks based on capacity
