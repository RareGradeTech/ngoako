About Us

Providing compassionate support and ensuring every detail is handled with the utmost care.

Company Overview

NGOAKO TOMBSTONE & FUNERAL SERVICES is a distinguished provider of tombstone design, manufacturing, and comprehensive funeral services. With a profound commitment to compassion, integrity, and personalized care, we help families navigate the challenging journey of bidding farewell to their loved ones with dignity and respect.

We provide comprehensive solutions to clients across various households and industries. What sets us apart is our commitment to providing comprehensive solutions under one roof—saving our clients time and effort in coordinating multiple service providers.

The company is 100% black-owned, and 100% black managed.

Vision

To be the service provider that is most highly regarded and relied upon. We want to earn a good name for ourselves in the community as a responsible employer, and we also want to have a stellar reputation among the companies we do business with.

Mission

Our goal is to provide an outstanding level of service delivery to each one of our customers across the entirety of South Africa. Our primary focus will be on enhancing public safety. We are going to set the company up so that it can become the most successful and most widely recognized brand in the funeral services.

Objectives & Quality Policy

  • To provide quality and professional services always.
  • To ensure customer satisfaction.
  • To create employment opportunities for local people.

Our dedication to excellence is ingrained in our culture. We prioritize Customer Satisfaction, maintain a culture of Quality Excellence, prioritize Compliance and Safety, and believe in the power of Continuous Improvement to drive our success.

Empowerment Policy

  • Workplace diversity and inclusion

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions under one roof, providing quality, reliability, and on-time delivery.

Tombstone Design & Manufacturing

Our unique range of affordable, high quality tombstones come in various colors and designs. They are made of SABS specs, having been designed to stand the test of time and elements.

  • Customized tombstones crafted from high-quality materials.
  • Personalized designs (engravings, photographs, epitaphs).
  • Various styles and finishes to suit individual preferences.
  • Markers for gravesites.

Funeral Planning & Arrangements

We assist families in planning and organizing funeral services tailored to cultural and religious preferences.

  • Professional coordination with cemeteries and crematoriums.
  • Embalming room equipment.
  • Caskets/Coffins & Urns supply.
  • Hearse / Van provision.
  • Decoration tools and flowers.
