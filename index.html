About Us

Providing compassionate support and ensuring every detail is handled with the utmost care.

Company Overview

NGOAKO TOMBSTONE & FUNERAL SERVICES is a distinguished provider of tombstone design, manufacturing, and comprehensive funeral services. With a profound commitment to compassion, integrity, and personalized care, we help families navigate the challenging journey of bidding farewell to their loved ones with dignity and respect.

What sets us apart is our commitment to providing comprehensive solutions under one roof—saving our clients time and effort. The company is 100% black-owned, and 100% black managed.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the service provider that is most highly regarded and relied upon. We want to earn a good name for ourselves in the community as a responsible employer, and we also want to have a stellar reputation among the companies we do business with.

Mission: Our goal is to provide an outstanding level of service delivery to each one of our customers across the entirety of South Africa, ensuring customers receive timely and high-quality service.

Objectives & Quality

Our dedication to excellence is ingrained in our culture. Our objectives are to provide quality services always, ensure customer satisfaction, and create local employment.

  • Customer Satisfaction: Actively seeking feedback to continually improve.
  • Quality Excellence: Fostering a mind-set of continuous improvement.
  • Compliance and Safety: Providing a safe working environment.

Empowerment Policy

We believe in building a workforce and business model that supports the growth and development of our community.

  • Black Economic Empowerment
  • Women Empowerment
  • Affirmative Action
  • Youth Empowerment
  • Workplace diversity and inclusion
